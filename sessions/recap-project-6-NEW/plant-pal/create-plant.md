# Create Plant

## Value Proposition

**As a user**

**I want to** create and store new plants,

**so that** I can personalise and expand the plant selection to better reflect my collection and preferences.

## Acceptance Criteria

- A form for adding plants is displayed at the top of the homepage.
- The form is headlined with its purpose.
- The form must include labelled fields for:
  - Plant name
  - Plant botanical name
  - Plant description
  - Plant light needs
  - Plant water needs
  - Plant fertiliser season
- The following fields are mandatory:
  - Plant name
  - Plant botanical name
  - Plant light needs
  - Plant water needs
- The light needs are selected via radio buttons with the options: Full Sun, Partial Shade, Full Shade.
- The water needs are selected via radio buttons with the options: Low, Medium, High.
- The fertiliser season is selected via checkboxes, allowing multiple selections with the options: Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter.
- Form submission with any empty mandatory fields is blocked, and clear validation messages indicate the fields that need completion.
- Upon form submission, the new plant is added to the top of the plant list.
