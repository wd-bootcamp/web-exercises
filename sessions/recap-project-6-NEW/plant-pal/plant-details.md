# Plant Details

## Value Proposition

**As a user**

**I want to** access detailed information about each plant,

**so that** I can get a better understanding of the plant's needs and care requirements.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Each plant in the plant list has a link to a details page.
- Each details page contains:
  - Plant name
  - Plant botanical name
  - Plant image
  - Plant description
  - Plant light needs
  - Plant water needs
  - Plant fertiliser season
- The water needs, light needs and fertiliser seasons are visualised with fitting icons (e.g. empty or full droplets of water).
- The detailed view includes a “back” link, allowing users to easily navigate back to the plant list.
